FAQ

Common questions.

If something isn't answered here, ask us directly — we mean it.

What does "pro bono publico" actually mean for pricing?
Our first engagements are offered at no cost as we build and validate the service. We're genuinely invested in your outcome — not because it's charity, but because it's how we prove what this can do.
What fields do you specialise in?
Our current focus is technical professionals: AI/ML, cloud infrastructure, DevOps, Healthcare IT, cybersecurity, and adjacent fields. If your work is technical and your job search is stuck, we're worth a conversation.
How is this different from a recruiter?
Recruiters work for employers. We work for you. We're not placing you — we're helping you navigate, prepare, and present yourself. We have no incentive to push you toward a role that doesn't fit.
What does the daily job digest look like?
A curated list of matching roles from across the web, filtered to your specific criteria — role type, location, salary range, company size, industry. Delivered daily so you're always working with fresh intelligence.
How much time will this take on my end?
We design around your real capacity — not an ideal one. Most clients spend 30–60 minutes a week in active sessions, plus whatever time they choose to put into applications. We make that time count.
What if I don't know exactly what role I'm looking for?
That's exactly what we start with. The Clarity Session is built for this. Most of our clients come in knowing they want something different — not yet knowing precisely what. That's fine. That's the work.
